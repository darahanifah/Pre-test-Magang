About

Cyber ​​Army Indonesia (CyberArmyID) is a company in the field of Information and Cyber ​​Security. Currently CyberArmyID focuses on Vulnerability Disclosure Program (VDP), ranging from connecting security researchers (Bughunters) to affected companies / organizations; manage reports of vulnerabilities; analyzing the impact; give rewards to Bughunters who have participated in reporting - and provide education such as technical training and awareness related to information and cyber security.

CyberArmyID also acts as the Vulnerability Coordination Center of the public sector whose results are expected to become an integrated Information and Cyber Security reference in Indonesia.
